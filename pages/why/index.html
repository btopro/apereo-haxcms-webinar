Why? Well... Why is WordPress easier for authors to understand than Drupal?

Why is Canvas easier to adopt then Sakai?

Why is SquareSpace so popular that people forget it locks them into that platform forever?

Why? Well, because authoring experience is everything. We're all so focused on end users that we forget we are the ones that picked a technical solution for a reason -- it was approachable TO US AND OUR USERS.

HAX seeks to make the best authoring experience on the web that is free of platform constraints .
