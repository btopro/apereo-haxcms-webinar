So if there's HAXeditor... then what's HAXcms you might ask (the name of the webinar...)?

HAXcms is a CMS built on the concept of what the lightest weight, static CMS could possibly be if...

  • We have access to the entire webcomponents.org library
  • We have HAXeditor as our editing experience
  • We leveraged both to create highly static, yet engaging websites

What you get is the static publishing capabilities of Jekyll, with the theming engine of powerful CMS, with simplicity greater than WordPress. It is a best of breeds solution that is a micro-site factory . HAXcms is intended to be leveraged to build new websites, and then publish them to a static format that mirrors the dynamic form yet no longer requires a dynamic backend in order to be delivered.

Where does that leave me...

The result is incredibly small websites that require no maintenance, have high scale (cause they are static...) and are easy to update and theme.
